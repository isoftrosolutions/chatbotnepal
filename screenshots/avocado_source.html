The Most Exclusive Accommodation in Hetauda

Welcome to the serene and enchanting Avocado and Orchid Resort, nestled amidst the breathtaking landscapes of Hetauda, Nepal. This exquisite resort is a true paradise for nature lovers, offering a perfect blend of tranquillity, luxury, and natural beauty. From the moment you arrive, you will be captivated by the resort's lush green surroundings, adorned with vibrant orchids and towering avocado trees.

Orchid Tours

If you have an interest in Orchids then you can find no better place to start your exploration other than Orchid garden. There are some 80 species in the cultivation and their central location allows access to 150 Orchids species in a natural setting.

Culinary delights await you at Avocado and Orchid Resort's restaurants, where our local chefs blend local flavours with international cuisine, creating a diverse and mouthwatering menu.

Savor each bite in the elegant dining areas or choose to dine al fresco, surrounded by the stunning vistas of the resort. Our menu reflects a variety of continental, Chinese, Indian and Nepalese dishes.
