# Avocado and Orchid Resort — Hotel Overview

## About the Resort
Avocado and Orchid Resort Pvt. Ltd. is a premier resort located in the heart of Hetauda, Makwanpur, Nepal. Founded in the 1980s, the resort offers a perfect blend of tranquillity, luxury, and natural beauty surrounded by lush green gardens, vibrant orchids, and towering avocado trees.

It is the **only resort in Nepal** with a dedicated Orchid Garden, featuring 80 cultivated species and access to 150 orchid species in their natural setting. The resort is consistently rated as the **#1 accommodation in Hetauda** on TripAdvisor.
