# Avocado and Orchid Resort — Amenities & Facilities

## Internet & Connectivity
- **Free Wi-Fi** available throughout the property (in-room and public areas)

## Parking
- **Free on-site private parking** — no reservation needed

## Front Desk & Security
- **24-hour front desk**
- Full-day security
- Safe deposit box at front desk
- Luggage storage available

## Laundry
- **Laundry services available** (charges apply)
- Self-serve laundry option also available

## Garden & Outdoor
- Beautiful lush green garden with manicured lawns
- **Orchid Garden** — the only one in Nepal (80+ cultivated species, access to 150 species in natural setting)
- Fresh Avocado trees on premises — only place in Nepal to enjoy fresh avocado fruit
- Garden seating and relaxation area

## Café & Bar
- On-site **Coffee Shop / Café**
- **Bar / Lounge** available for guests

## Electric Vehicle
- **Electric vehicle charging point** available

## Other Amenities
- Daily housekeeping
- Room service (6:30 AM – 9:45 PM)
- Front-desk safe
- Iron / Ironing board (in rooms)

## What's NOT Available
- Swimming pool — **Not available**
- Spa — **Not available**
- In-house gym — **Not available**

## Unique Experiences
- **Orchid Garden Tour** — Explore 80+ orchid species on-site; wild orchid tours can also be arranged
- **Fresh Avocado** — Taste fresh avocado fruit and signature guacamole, unique to this resort in Nepal
- **Nature & Garden Walks** — Acres of greenery, manicured lawns, and natural beauty
