# Avocado and Orchid Resort — Hotel Policies

## Check-in & Check-out
- **Check-in Time:** 2:00 PM
- **Check-out Time:** 12:00 PM (Noon)
- Guests arriving after 10:00 PM should inform the hotel in advance

## Cancellation Policy
| Cancellation Timing | Charge |
|---|---|
| 48–72 hours before arrival | **Free cancellation** — No charge |
| 24–48 hours before arrival | **50%** of total booking amount |
| Within 24 hours OR No-show | **100%** of total booking amount |

## ID & Documentation
- ID/Documents required for **1 person only** (government-issued photo ID)

## Extra Bed
- Extra bed available at **Rs. 1,000 per night**

## Smoking Policy
- The property is **100% smoke-free**

## Pet Policy
- **Pets are not allowed**

## Children Policy
- Children of all ages are welcome
- Children aged 6 and above may be charged as adults
- Please contact the hotel regarding infant/crib requirements

## Payment
- Payment before arrival by bank transfer may be required
- The hotel will contact you after booking with payment instructions

## General
- The resort reserves the right to decline service to anyone violating hotel rules
- Guests are responsible for any damage caused to hotel property
