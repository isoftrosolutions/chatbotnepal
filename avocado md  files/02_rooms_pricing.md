# Avocado and Orchid Resort — Rooms & Pricing

## Room Types and Rates

All prices are in Nepalese Rupees (NPR). VAT is included in all rates.

| Room Type | EP (Room Only) | BB (Bed & Breakfast) |
|---|---|---|
| Deluxe A/C Single | Rs. 2,500 | Rs. 3,000 |
| Deluxe A/C Double | Rs. 3,000 | Rs. 4,000 |
| Super Deluxe A/C Single | Rs. 3,500 | Rs. 4,000 |
| Super Deluxe A/C Double | Rs. 4,000 | Rs. 5,000 |

**EP** = European Plan (Room only, no meals)
**BB** = Bed & Breakfast (Room + Breakfast included)

## Extra Bed
- Extra Bed: **Rs. 1,000 per night**

## Total Rooms
- The resort operates **58 rooms** — all equally clean and comfortable with full services and facilities.

## Room Categories Description

### Deluxe A/C Rooms
Clean, comfortable and well-equipped rooms with all standard amenities. Ideal for solo travelers or couples seeking quality accommodation at an affordable price.

### Super Deluxe A/C Rooms
Upgraded rooms with more space, enhanced furnishings, and premium amenities. Perfect for guests wanting a more luxurious stay.
