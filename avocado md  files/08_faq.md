## Rooms & Pricing
**Q: Is breakfast included?**
A: Only in BB (Bed & Breakfast) rate rooms. EP (European Plan) rooms do not include breakfast — it can be ordered separately from the restaurant.

**Q: Are pets allowed?**
A: No pets allowed.

**Q: Is the property smoke-free?**
A: Yes, 100% smoke-free.

---

## Facilities
**Q: Is Wi-Fi free?**
A: Yes, free Wi-Fi throughout the resort including all rooms.

**Q: Is there a swimming pool, spa, or gym?**
A: No — the resort does not have a swimming pool, spa, or in-house gym.

**Q: Is parking available?**
A: Yes, free on-site private parking. No reservation needed. Electric vehicle charging point available.

**Q: Do you have an Orchid Garden?**
A: Yes — the only dedicated Orchid Garden at any resort in Nepal (80+ cultivated species).

## Dining
**Q: What are the restaurant hours?**
A: Avocado Restaurant is open 6:00 AM – 10:00 PM. Room service runs 6:30 AM – 9:45 PM.

**Q: When are BBQ and Tandoor items available?**
A: After 6:00 PM only.

**Q: Do you have a bar?**
A: Yes, full bar with domestic spirits, international spirits, wine, and beer.
